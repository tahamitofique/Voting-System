Admin Login



How to use?


If you have account already, then SIGN IN by entering your username and password.

If you don't have an account, then SIGN UP. Fill the required information and get registered for voting by signing up for your account.

After logging into your account, you can vote for your favorite candidate by clicking under a respective candidate.

You can vote only ONCE and once you have voted, you cannot change your vote so think wisely before voting.

When you are done with the voting process, you can check the voting results. Then logout from your account.
